Our Donate Plan

Sylhet Division area of Bangladesh is under water due to heavy flood. People are suffering badly. They need shelter, food, medical support and drinking water. Please raise your hand to donate for those victims. People need our support. Here are the details of the plan....

Food for everyone

We aim to make essentials like food, medicine and clean drinking water available by providing the flood victims with grocery packages.like sugar, biscuits, candles, match box, sanitary napkin, orsaline, water purification tablet etc.

Education for child

In shelters we will try to provide primary education to the child. We will also try to provide them some essentials educational material. Primary education is very important to all of us so we will aware them about it

Shelter for a family

When the flood recedes and conditions become more favorable for this part of our project, we plan to providing shelter for those who lost them. By supplying them with building materials, providing monetary aid and rebuilding damaged houses.
